JOLLOF

Description

This is a recipe made undoubtedly better than no other country but Ghana

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Tomato puree & tomato paste
  • Salt, black pepper, cayenne pepper, rosemary, scotch bonnet
  • Onions,ginger & garlic
  • Cooking oil
  • A pound of chicken
  • Carrots, green beans, ball pepper

Steps

  1. Pour oil into large saucepan. Cook onion in oil over medium-low heat until translucent.
  2. Stir in tomato puree and tomato paste, and season with salt, black pepper, cayenne pepper, and rosemary. Cover, and bring to a boil. Reduce heat, stir in water, and add chicken pieces. Simmer for 30 minutes.
  3. Stir in rice, carrots, and green beans, and ball pepper. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to low. Cover, and simmer until the chicken is fork-tender and the rice is cooked, 25 to 30 minutes.
