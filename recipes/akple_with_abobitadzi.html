Akple with Abobitadi

Description

This is a recipe made undoubtedly better than no other country but Ghana

Ingredients

  • Corn Flour
  • Cassava Dough
  • Abobi (Dried Anchovies)
  • Tomatoes
  • Scotch bonnet
  • Onions
  • Ginger
  • Garlic
  • Salt

Steps

Making of Akple

  1. Pour two cups of water into a clean cooking pot and place it on fire
  2. Wait for the water to boil and add a reasonable amount of salt
  3. Pour desired amount of the corn flour into the boiling water in bits to ensure there is consistency
  4. Mix desired amount of cassava dough in warm water and add to the mixture. make sure you use at most one-third cassava dough as compared to the corn flour.
  5. Using your cleaned spatula, stir the mixture continuously to ensure a uniform and consistent paste is formed.
  6. Continue stirring until the akple is well cooked.
  7. Mold it into serving bowls or storage containers and store temporarily.

Making of Abobitadi

  1. Put the abobi in a pan and place it on fire to roast for 5 minutes maximum
  2. Make a salt solution and soaked the roasted abobi in it
  3. Pour a few ounces of oil in the pan to heat and then add the abobi, fry for a little bit
  4. Grind the tomatoes, onion, scotch bonnet, garlic and ginger together
  5. Now add the fried abobi to the grinded mixture and serve
